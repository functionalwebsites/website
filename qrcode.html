QR Code Generator

This is a good QR code generator. Some shady websites will add a gateway website to their QR codes so that once you've printed your marketing materials with their QR codes, they can require you to pay them for it to continue working. By adding a gateway, or an in-between website, they can direct traffic to their website before they direct it to yours. So whenever they choose, they can turn off the redirect and force people to pay to continue redirecting traffic via the QR code. It's nasty!
